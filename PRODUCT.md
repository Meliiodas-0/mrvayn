# MrVayn — Product Context

> Audience, positioning, and voice for the portfolio. Companion to `DESIGN.md`.

## What this is

A personal portfolio for **MrVayn** — game developer (Co-Founder & CTO,
Magadha Studios). A single-page, cinematic showcase of journey, projects, and
collaborations, with an interactive "shoot the rockets" mini-game woven in.

## Audience

- **Studios / collaborators** evaluating craft (Unreal Engine 5, multiplayer,
  Niagara VFX, cutscenes, shipping).
- **Investors / partners** gauging ambition and traction.
- **Community / peers** from games and esports.

Primary action: start a conversation (the "Let's Talk" / "Send Message" CTAs to
email). Secondary: explore projects and proof points.

## Positioning

Premium, technical, and a little playful. The work should read as
"ships real, scalable, cinematic games" — backed by concrete proof (IGDC 2025,
Couragely launch metrics, shipped roles) rather than buzzwords.

## Voice

- Confident and concrete; numbers over adjectives.
- Game-native and cinematic, never corporate-bland.
- First person, direct ("I build…"), warm but precise.

## Content priorities (in order)

1. Who he is + headline craft (Hero, About).
2. Track record (Journey timeline, Featured Projects + proof metrics).
3. Reach (Collaborations).
4. Contact / availability.

## Non-goals

- Not a blog or docs site. Not a generic agency template.
- The mini-game and 3D are atmosphere — they must never bury the content.
